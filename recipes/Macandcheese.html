3 Ingredient Mac and Cheese

Yummo!

Nothing says comforting food and classic like mac and cheese. Remember when you were a little kid and you sat down to the dinner table? There it was: a glorious pot of mac and cheese. Ooey, gooey and oh-so-delightful.

Well, we're taking you back with our 3 ingredient mac-hack. That's right: only three ingredients to bliss. It's so good, you'll never go back to the boxed stuff!

Ingredients

  • 5 cups milk (1 1/4 L)
  • 1lb elbow macaroni (445g)
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese (200g)

    • Steps

    1. In a large pot, bring the milk to a boil
    2. Add the pasta and stir constantly until pasta is cooked, about 10 minutes
    3. Turn off the heat, then add the cheddar. Stir until the cheese is melted and the pasta is evenly coated.
    4. Enjoy!
