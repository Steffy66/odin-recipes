Whiskey Peach

Noice!

This content is intended solely for users of legal drinking age

Drink responsibly.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup ice (70g)
  • 1 tblspn peach jam
  • 2 tblspns lemon juice
  • 2fl oz whiskey (60ml)
  • lemon twist, for garnish

    • Steps

    1. In a cocktail shaker, combine the ice, peach jam, lemon juice and whiskey.
    2. Cover with the lid and shake vigorously for 30 seconds.
    3. Strain into desired serving glass.
    4. Garnish with lemon twist.
    5. Enjoy!
